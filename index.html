Did You Know?

Product or Service Fast Facts
Communication Equipment As compared to 1959, we are spending 340,000% more on communication equipments today.
Membership Club Membership club performed the worst during the COVID-19 health and economic crisis, suffering a 77% drop in revenue during the second quarter of 2020.

To put into perspective, the second largest drop in revenue was in 2009, a 2.52% drop.
Oil Industries: Fuel Oil & Motor Vehicle (oil, lubricants, etc) The most volatile industry across the 1980-2020 period.
Animal feeds/Farm supplies Spending on this category suffered a drop in revenue every year for at least 10 years before recovering in March 2004. The recovery only lasted 3 years.

Today, we are only spending 0.36x the amount spend in 1959 on Animal feeds/Farm supplies.

For any investors that have invested in this industry, YUCK!





Normal Recession vs COVID-19

Product or Service Fast Facts
Normal Recession Losers The greatest losers in a normal economic recessions are often durable goods such as Motor vehicles, Furnitures and furnishings, and Sport and recreational vehicles.

Are they the greatest losers in the year 2020 of COVID-19 health and economic crisis?
Normal Recession Proof Convential economic wisdoms suggest that product/service categories such as health care, accomodation and food services are generally recession proof (i.e. Consumer continues to buy them during a recession).

Did you know that health care did not suffer a drop in revenue even under all under economic conditions in the past? Is this still the case in the 2020 COVID-19 health and economic crisis? Check it!
COVID-19 Winners Other than increase in spending by Nonprofit institutions, the products and services that have enjoyed the greatest boost from the health crisis are Sports and recreational vehicles, Sporting equipments and guns, Alcoholic beverages, House and gardening tools and recreational items.
